ABOUT THIS ARCHIVE

This library is part of the early stages of my thesis process—a collection of references, provocations, and fragments of research. More than the resources themselves, I'm interested in how the vessel that holds them changes their meaning. Here you can move between grid, mess, and spatial views: three modes that reflect different ways of learning and making.

The grid offers clarity and order, the mess captures the unfinished energy of a working studio, and the spatial view turns navigation itself into a form of inquiry. Together, these modes show how form is never neutral—the structure of an archive is always a kind of pedagogy.
