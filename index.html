Open Studio Thesis Documentation by Hyning Gan

Welcome to Open Studio. This site is an open-source notebook for my Parsons CD thesis process, documenting research, references, and experiments in real time. By sharing the process of developing my personal practice, I'm exploring design as a shared commons—– and invite others to learn, question, and build with me.
